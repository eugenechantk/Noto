if you want to validate PMF in 30 days, do this:

context:

in 2021, we raised millions to make a wearable device - but people struggled using it and it shut down.

meanwhile, flow - the software powering the device - started spreading on its own. since then, we've raised over $81M.

here's how to build something people want:

1/ people keep using it

not just the first week, then dropping off. people made it part of their daily routine. we saw users talking to Flow hundreds of times per day.

2/ people feel strongly about it

one user who's paralyzed from the neck down cut his email writing time from 30 minutes to one minute. people posted about Flow on social media without us asking. that's when you know it actually matters.

3/ people tell other people

we went from 2 users to thousands in a month. no advertising. just people recommending it to others.

4/ people pay for it

when we introduced a paid version, 20% of users upgraded right away. that's 5 times higher than normal. people pay when you solve a real problem for them.

if you're building something right now, ask yourself: which of these four signs do you actually see?

if you can't point to clear proof of at least two, you might be building the wrong thing.
